# Data Analyst Portfolio – Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow, transforming raw sales data into actionable business insights using Excel, PostgreSQL, SQL, and Power BI.

The analysis is based on a retail sales dataset sourced from Kaggle, containing transactional sales records across multiple years, regions, customer segments, products, discounts, and profitability metrics. The objective was to analyze business performance, identify trends, and provide insights that can support strategic decision-making and improve profitability.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations generate large volumes of sales data, but data alone does not drive business success. Decision-makers require clear insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends in order to make informed business decisions.

This project seeks to answer critical business questions, including:

* What were the total sales generated during the analysis period?
* What profit was generated, and what was the overall profit margin?
* How did sales and profits trend over time?
* Which product categories and products performed best and worst?
* What impact did discounts have on profitability?
* Which regions and states contributed most to sales and profit?
* Which products generated losses despite producing sales?
* How can the business optimize product strategy and pricing decisions?

## Challenges Encountered

During development, several real-world data challenges were encountered and resolved:

* Date format inconsistencies during PostgreSQL import
* CSV formatting and quotation mark issues
* Data import errors
* Database-to-Power BI connection configuration
* Data type validation and transformation

Resolving these challenges provided valuable experience in data cleaning, troubleshooting, and workflow management—skills that are essential in professional analytics environments.
